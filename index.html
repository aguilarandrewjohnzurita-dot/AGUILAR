About Me

I am Andrew John Aguilar, born on April 13, 2006 in Laste, Magsaysay, Occidental Mindoro. I come from a simple and hardworking family—my father, Andy T. Aguilar, is both a fisherman and a farmer, while my mother, Admerlyn Z. Aguilar, is a nutritionist. I have four siblings: my brothers Adrian Jay Z. Aguilar (an Army soldier), Andy Z. Aguilar, Zwing Lhian Z. Aguilar, and my sister Axel Jane Z. Aguilar. I also have pets named Putol, Rocky, Owen, and Meyo. I studied in Laste Elementary School, Bagong Sikat Elementary School, San Jose National High School, Magsaysay National High School – Calawag Extension, and Magsaysay National High School. I first took BSEE at Occidental Mindoro State College – Labangan Campus before shifting to BSIT at OMSC San Jose Campus. I’m a quiet person, and my role model is my father. My hobbies include fishing, drawing, and playing chess, and fishing is also my sport as an angler. My favorite things include the color red, foods like tinola, sinigang, and chicken curry, the music groups Westlife and Backstreet Boys, and my favorite Westlife song is “I Wanna Grow Old With You.” I enjoy watching Harry Potter, and my favorite actor and actress are Daniel Radcliffe and Emma Watson. I love eating at karinderya, and one memorable place I visited was Tagaytay, where I competed in Electrical Installation and Maintenance. In school, I enjoy web designing, and my favorite subject is Web System, though I don’t like the stress from deadlines. For fun, I love to go fishing or draw. After college, I plan to work—possibly even abroad in Japan. One quote I live by is: “Be yourself.”
