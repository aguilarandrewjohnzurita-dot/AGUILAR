Chicken Curry Recipe

By Andrew John Z. Aguilar

Ingredients (Serves 4–6)

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced or grated
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped or pureed
  • 2-3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit (optional)
  • 1 cup coconut milk or yogurt
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped, for garnish
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Wash the chicken pieces and pat them dry. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden brown. Then add garlic, ginger, and green chilies; cook 1–2 minutes.
  4. Stir in turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, paprika, and black pepper. Add tomatoes and cook until oil separates from the masala (5–7 mins).
  5. Add chicken pieces and stir well. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Add coconut milk or yogurt and water (adjust thickness). Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20–25 minutes until chicken is cooked.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala, stir gently, and garnish with chopped coriander.
  8. Serve hot with rice, roti, or naan.
